<Section icon={Swords} title="Repulsorcraft Classes and Physics Mastery">

Success in Star Wars Galactic Racer vr requires a deep understanding of the four primary repulsorcraft classes. Each vehicle type behaves differently within the VR environment, demanding unique physical movements and strategic approaches to cornering and combat.

| Class | Handling | Top Speed | Durability | Best Environment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Podracer** | Challenging | **Extreme** | High | Open deserts / Long straights |
| **Speeder Bike** | **Excellent** | High | Very Low | Tight urban tunnels / Jungles |
| **Landspeeder** | Average | Moderate | **Very High** | Combat-heavy circuit races |
| **Skim Speeder** | Good | Fast | Moderate | Technical tracks with elevation |

Each class offers a distinct playstyle. For instance, the Speeder Bike allows for agile maneuvers and the ability to drive up the sides of tunnels—a tactic that is impossible for the larger, more cumbersome Podracers. However, one wrong move in a Speeder Bike often results in a total wreck due to its low durability.
