<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Roguelite Loop: Galactic Tour Rules">

The core of the story campaign is the Galactic Tour, a runs-based progression system that demands consistent performance and strategic resource management. To participate in a tour, players must risk a League Entry Token. This token acts as your ticket to the tournament; losing it forces you to return to the qualifiers and begin a fresh run.

During a tour, you will navigate a branching map of events across various Outer Rim planets. Each node on the map presents different race types, rewards, and potential hazards. Maintaining a top-three placement is crucial; falling behind or completely wrecking your vehicle can result in tour failure. However, some resources, such as Galactic Credits, carry over between runs, allowing you to purchase permanent upgrades and parts from paddock vendors.

| Tour Phase | Focus | Failure State | Key Resource |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Qualifier | Secure entry into the league | Lose entry token | Galactic Credits |
| Branching Tour | Route selection and heats | Vehicle destruction | Upgrade parts |
| Championship | Challenge Kestar Bool | Title loss | Galactic Glory |

<Callout type="warning" title="Risk Management">
Do not spend all your Galactic Credits on temporary boosts. Always retain a baseline fund to ensure you can purchase replacement parts if your vehicle suffers critical damage during a high-risk heat.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Your First Galactic Tour Run">

Succeeding in your initial campaign run requires a balance of aggressive driving, careful route planning, and timely mechanical upgrades. Follow this step-by-step progression path to optimize your early-game performance.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize Your Tour">
    Acquire your first League Entry Token and select your starting chassis. Beginners should opt for the Landspeeder to maximize survival rates during early contact-heavy races.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Map Your Route Strategically">
    Analyze the branching tour map. Look for nodes that offer high Galactic Credit payouts or specific upgrade parts rather than simply taking the shortest path to the finals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Heat and Boost Mechanics">
    Use your Afterburner for repeatable, safe speed increases. Reserve the high-risk Ramjet boost for overtaking rivals on straightaways or executing aggressive tactical shunts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spend Credits in the Paddock">
    Between races, visit the paddock area. Interact with other pilots, buy performance parts from vendors, and prioritize upgrading your vehicle's Cornering and Battery stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Secure a Top-Three Finish">
    Avoid unnecessary risks in the final laps. Maintaining a podium finish is vital to retaining your League Entry Token and keeping the run alive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Paddock Interactions">
Take the time to talk to NPC pilots in the paddock. Some characters offer unique side objectives or trade rare vehicle components that cannot be bought from standard vendors.
</Callout>

</Section>
