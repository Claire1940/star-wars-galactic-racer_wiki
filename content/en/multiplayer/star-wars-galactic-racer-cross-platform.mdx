<Section icon={Swords} title="Crossplay Mechanics & Network Architecture">

Understanding how the cross-platform system operates is key to optimizing your multiplayer experience. The network infrastructure utilizes centralized game accounts to manage player profiles, progression, and friends lists across different storefronts and consoles. 

On PC, the game features Denuvo Anti-Tamper technology to maintain competitive integrity and prevent cheating in online PvP environments [1]. This anti-cheat framework operates alongside the cross-play network to ensure console players experience fair matchmaking when paired with PC racers.

| Platform | Crossplay Support | Controller Support | Input Type |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| PC (Steam) | Yes, fully supported | Yes, highly recommended | Keyboard or Controller |
| PlayStation | Yes, fully supported | Yes, native support | Controller |
| Xbox | Yes, fully supported | Yes, native support | Controller |

While keyboard and mouse inputs are fully functional on PC, the physics engine is heavily optimized for analog input. Utilizing a controller provides much finer throttle management and steering adjustments, which are critical when navigating tight corridors at breakneck speeds [1].

<Callout type="warning" title="Denuvo Activation Limit">

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Set Up Cross-Platform Lobbies">

Connecting with friends on other platforms is a straightforward process. By utilizing the in-game social menu, you can bypass platform-specific limitations and jump straight into the action.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Social Menu">
    Launch the game and navigate to the main menu. Select the Social tab to open your friends list and managing options.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Your Galactic League ID">
    Your unique Galactic League ID is displayed at the top of the Social panel. This ID consists of your profile name followed by a unique string of numbers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Add Cross-Platform Friends">
    Select 'Add Friend' and input your friend's Galactic League ID. Once they accept the pending request, they will appear in your cross-platform friends list.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Create and Invite to Lobby">
    Create a custom multiplayer lobby, select your desired game mode, and invite your friends directly from your in-game friends list, regardless of their platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once your lobby is assembled, the host can configure custom rules, select tracks from various Outer Rim planets, and toggle AI filler racers on or off to customize the competitive environment.

<Callout type="tip" title="Voice Chat Settings">
Ensure in-game voice chat is enabled in your audio settings if you are playing with friends on different platforms, as system-level party chats (like Discord or console parties) may require external setup.
