<Section icon={Shield} title="Tracks & Planets: Navigating the Outer Rim Circuits">

The Galactic League takes competitors across a diverse range of worlds, from familiar desert sands to treacherous frozen wastes. Each planet offers unique environmental hazards and track layouts that require different driving techniques. Memorizing these tracks and adapting your chassis choice to the terrain is vital for maintaining a podium position.

On Tatooine, racers will navigate the iconic Mos Espa Circuit, where high-speed podracing is the main attraction. This track features narrow canyon passes, sharp caves, and hazardous shortcuts that reward high-risk maneuvers. Conversely, Ando Prime presents a frozen landscape where low-traction ice surfaces make drifting incredibly dangerous. Players must rely on precise banking turns to avoid colliding with the icy barriers.

<Callout type="info" title="Environmental Hazards">
Always check the planet profile before a race. Slippery ice on Ando Prime or volcanic ash on Sentinel I drastically alter handling physics.
</Callout>

Other notable locations include Jakku, an unsanctioned scrapyard circuit cluttered with military wreckage and dusty dunes. Lantaana offers a lush jungle setting with blind corners hidden by dense foliage, while Sentinel I features a hostile volcanic surface where players must dodge active lava flows and falling debris.
