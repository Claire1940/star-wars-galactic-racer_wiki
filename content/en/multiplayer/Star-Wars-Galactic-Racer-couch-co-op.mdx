<Section icon={Map} title="How to Setup Multiplayer Races">

Setting up a session to play with friends is straightforward, thanks to the integrated social menus and cross-platform architecture. Follow these steps to get your squad into the Outer Rim circuits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Multiplayer Menu">
    From the main hangar, navigate to the "Multiplayer" tab. Here, you can choose between public matchmaking or creating a private "Grudge Match" lobby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invite Friends via Cross-Network ID">
    Use the in-game social tool to invite friends. Since the game supports cross-play, you can add friends using their unique Secret Mode ID, regardless of whether they are on PC, PS5, or Xbox.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Repulsorcraft">
    Each player chooses their chassis class: Landspeeder, Speeder Bike, Skim Speeder, or Podracer. Ensure your loadout is optimized for the selected track's environment (e.g., Ice for Ando Prime or Dust for Jakku).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Configure Race Rules">
    In private lobbies, the host can select the planet, track hazards, and the number of laps. You can also toggle "Contact Damage" and "Takedown Rewards" to customize the intensity of the race.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Note">
When playing in a group, coordinate your vehicle choices. Having a mix of "heavy" Landspeeders for shunting and "fast" Speeder Bikes for overtakes can make team-based community events much more strategic.
</Callout>
