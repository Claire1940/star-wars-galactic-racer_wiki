<Section icon={Swords} title="Competitive PvP Mechanics and Combat">

Success in Star Wars Galactic Racer multiplayer requires more than just raw speed. The game emphasizes "contact racing," where aggressive maneuvers are rewarded. Pilots must master the balance between maintaining a clean racing line and using their vehicle as a weapon to displace opponents.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Tactical Shunts">
    - **Side-swiping** rivals to force them into hazards.
    - Effective for defending narrow corridors.
    - Risk of losing momentum if timed poorly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Takedown System">
    - Completely disabling an opponent's craft.
    - Rewards players with **Ramjet energy**.
    - Essential for thinning the pack in 12-player races.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Overheat Management">
    - Balancing Afterburner and Ramjet usage.
    - Excessive boosting leads to temporary engine failure.
    - Critical during the final lap sprint.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The "Grudge Match" system tracks your interactions with other players across multiple sessions. If you frequently target a specific player for takedowns, the game acknowledges this rivalry, potentially increasing the rewards for defeating them in future encounters. This adds a layer of social strategy to the standard racing formula.

| Combat Mechanic | Input Trigger | Effect on Rival | Strategic Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shunt** | Lateral Input + Action | Physical displacement | Forces errors and missed gates |
| **Ramjet Burst** | Boost Modifier | High-impact collision | High chance of instant Takedown |
| **Kinetic Burst** | Chassis Trait (Bike) | Energy shockwave | Disrupts nearby steering controls |
| **Knife Edge** | Chassis Trait (Skim) | Precision cutting | Allows for aggressive interior lines |

<Callout type="warning" title="Risk of Failure">
Aggressive racing increases your **Crash Risk**. In multiplayer, crashing out doesn't just end your race; it can negatively impact your League reputation and lower your standing for future matchmaking.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Vehicle Customization for Competition">

In Star Wars Galactic Racer multiplayer, your vehicle's build is just as important as your driving skill. The game features four primary repulsorcraft classes, each with a distinct handling profile and "Known Trait" that can be upgraded using Galactic Credits earned during races.

| Vehicle Class | Handling Profile | Known Trait | Best Multiplayer Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Landspeeder** | Weighty / Drifting | **Drift** | Defensive play and tight corners |
| **Speeder Bike** | High Speed / Fragile | **Kinetic Burst** | Straight-line speed and overtakes |
| **Skim Speeder** | Technical / Banking | **Knife Edge** | Advanced routes and shortcuts |
| **Podracer** | Extreme Speed | **Podracing** | High-speed arena mastery |

Customization extends beyond performance. Players can equip unique **Liveries** and **Player Banners** to stand out in the lobby. Pre-ordering any edition of the game grants access to an exclusive bonus Livery and Banner, while the Deluxe Edition includes the "Kor Sarun" vehicle set, featuring three exclusive repulsorcraft designs.
