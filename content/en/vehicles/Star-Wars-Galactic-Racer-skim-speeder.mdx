<Section icon={Map} title="Planet-Specific Racing Strategies">

The Galactic League spans five primary planets, each offering distinct environmental hazards that affect how the **Star Wars Galactic Racer skim speeder** handles. Mastering these tracks requires a combination of memorization and reactive piloting.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Ando Prime Preparation">
    Focus on **Traction Control** upgrades. The icy surfaces reduce the effectiveness of standard repulsor fields, leading to unpredictable sliding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Tatooine Navigation">
    Watch for sandstorms that obscure visibility. Skim speeders can use their low profile to stay beneath the heaviest dust clouds near the canyon floors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Jakku Scavenging">
    The debris-heavy tracks of Jakku require high **Agility**. Use your skim speeder's vertical thrusters to jump over smaller wreckage piles that would stop a landspeeder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sentinel I Strategy">
    This urban circuit features many 90-degree turns. Practice the **Repulsor-Brake** technique to pivot around buildings without losing your momentum.
  </Step>
</Steps>
