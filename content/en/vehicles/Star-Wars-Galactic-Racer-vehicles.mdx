<Section icon={Swords} title="Star Wars Galactic Racer Vehicles: Core Classes & Handling">

The pilot roster of the Outer Rim demands specialized machinery to survive the high-stakes tournaments of the Galactic League. In *Star Wars: Galactic Racer*, players pilot various **Star Wars Galactic Racer vehicles** across a runs-based solo campaign and competitive online multiplayer. Choosing the correct vehicle class is the foundation of your racing career, directly dictating how you handle high-speed collisions, tight corners, and straight-line speed bursts.

The game features four distinct vehicle classes, each defined by a unique handling profile, weight distribution, and signature class trait. Landspeeders offer a heavier, drift-centric control scheme that is highly forgiving for beginners. Speeder bikes trade defense for raw, linear acceleration, requiring precise throttle management. Skim speeders utilize banking physics for technical, knife-edge cornering, while podracers deliver extreme, high-risk speeds optimized for wide-open arenas and iconic circuits like Mos Espa.

<Section icon={Target} title="Performance Upgrades & Customization Systems">

The core loop of the solo campaign relies on a runs-based roguelite structure. As Shade, you will navigate the Galactic Tour, earning Galactic Credits and acquiring performance parts between races. During paddock downtime, you can visit local vendors to purchase parts, talk with rival pilots, and install critical upgrades to prepare your vehicle for the escalating challenges of the higher league tiers.

Upgrades are divided into several key performance categories. Balancing these components is vital, as over-investing in raw speed without upgrading your cornering capabilities will quickly result in catastrophic crashes against track barriers.

| Upgrade Component | Stat Modification | Tactical Advantage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Afterburner** | Boost Efficiency | Increases the duration of standard speed boosts |
| **Battery** | Energy Capacity | Allows more frequent use of active abilities |
| **Cornering** | Lateral Traction | Reduces sliding and improves steering response |
| **Ramjet** | Burst Acceleration | Delivers high-risk speed bursts for takedowns |

To successfully optimize your vehicle during a campaign run, follow this structured upgrade path:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Chassis">
    Choose a base chassis (Landspeeder, Speeder Bike, or Skim Speeder) at the start of your Galactic Tour based on the initial track lineup.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Cornering">
    Prioritize early Cornering upgrades. High speed is useless if you cannot navigate the narrow corridors of Jakku or Tatooine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Expand Battery Capacity">
    Upgrade your Battery to ensure you have enough energy reserves to power your Afterburner and defensive shields during crowded pack races.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Tune the Ramjet">
    Apply Ramjet upgrades once you reach higher tiers. This allows you to aggressively shunt and takedown high-priority rivals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Apply Cosmetic Liveries">
    Personalize your upgraded machine with unlocked liveries and banners before entering multiplayer PvP lobbies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Overheating Danger">
Using the Ramjet boost delivers unmatched acceleration but rapidly builds engine heat. Triggering it repeatedly without cooling upgrades will overheat your engines, leaving you vulnerable to rival takedowns.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Track Hazards & Vehicle Synergy">

Races in the Galactic League take place across the lawless Outer Rim, featuring highly hazardous environments that can easily wreck unprepared vehicles. Matching your vehicle class to the specific environmental hazards of each planet is a crucial strategic layer in *Star Wars: Galactic Racer*. 

From the shifting sands of Jakku to the frozen, low-traction turns of Ando Prime, every location requires a different approach to handling and speed management.

| Location | Primary Hazard | Recommended Class | Strategy Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jakku** | Metal debris, narrow canyon paths | Landspeeder | Use heavy armor to survive scraping walls |
| **Tatooine** | High-speed straights, sand drifts | Podracer | Maximize Afterburner efficiency |
| **Ando Prime** | Slippery ice surfaces, zero traction | Skim Speeder | Rely on Knife Edge banking to avoid sliding |
| **Lantaana** | Dense jungle roots, blind corners | Speeder Bike | Use quick kinetic bursts to recover speed |
| **Sentinel I** | Volcanic heat, rapid engine buildup | Landspeeder | Prioritize cooling and battery upgrades |

Before committing your League Entry Token to a new tournament run, ensure your garage is fully prepared for the upcoming planetary environments.
