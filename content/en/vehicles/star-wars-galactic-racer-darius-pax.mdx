<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Who is Darius Pax? Character Lore & Profile">

Darius Pax is one of the most charismatic and influential figures operating within the underground racing scene of the Outer Rim [2]. Belonging to the multi-limbed Besalisk species, Pax possesses a boisterous, commanding voice that perfectly matches his larger-than-life personality [1, 2]. Unlike legitimate sports coordinators in the core worlds, Darius Pax runs the Galactic League, an unsanctioned, high-stakes racing circuit that operates hidden from the watchful eyes of the New Republic authorities [2]. 

His primary motivation stems from a deep-seated desire to reclaim the integrity of his league. The circuit has fallen under the ruthless grip of Kestar Bool, a powerful and corrupt champion who seeks to weaponize the Galactic League for his own power-hungry ambitions [2]. To counter this threat, Darius scouted Shade, a mysterious pilot with an unresolved grudge against the Bool family, offering a partnership that sets the entire campaign of Star Wars: Galactic Racer into motion [2].

<Section icon={Trophy} title="How to Progress: Step-by-Step Campaign Guide">

Starting your journey under Darius Pax requires proving your worth on the track [2]. The campaign begins with a simple delivery run that quickly escalates into a full-blown racing career [1]. Players must learn to handle the unique physics of the speeders, manage boost mechanics, and utilize combat options to survive.

Follow this step-by-step progression path to establish your name in the Galactic League and earn the trust of Darius Pax:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deliver the Consignment">
    Arrive at the Galactic League hangar alongside Jax to deliver a mysterious consignment cargo directly to Darius Pax [1, 2]. This initial delivery serves as your introduction to the hangar hub.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Accept the Exhibition Race">
    Darius will invite you to participate in a "friendly" exhibition race using the speeder you just delivered [1, 2]. Accept this invitation to unlock your very first playable vehicle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strip the Brakes with Evie">
    Consult with your mechanic, Evie, in the hangar hub [1]. She will suggest stripping the brakes off your speeder to drastically increase your top speed, introducing the high-risk drift mechanics [1].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter the Galactic League Circuit">
    Complete the exhibition race to officially register as a league pilot [1, 2]. Darius Pax will then open up the Outer Rim map, allowing you to select regional races and target Kestar Bool's lieutenants [2].
  </Step>
</Steps>
