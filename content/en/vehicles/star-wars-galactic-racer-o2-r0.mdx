<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Racing Strategies">

In Star Wars Galactic Racer, victory isn't just about crossing the finish line; it's about making sure your rivals don't. The **star wars galactic racer o2-r0** is built for "bullying." This involves using your speeder's mass to force opponents into walls or environmental hazards like the "Green Water" pits.

| Tactic | Description | Best Used Against | Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| The Bully | Physical side-swiping | Light Scouts | Wall Slam Takedown |
| Stealth Bait | Cloak and Brake | Aggressive Bulls | Rear-end Collision |
| Pit Maneuver | Tap rear thruster | Heavy Speeders | Spin-out |

Combat in the Galactic League is governed by the backers. Showing off for the backers by performing risky maneuvers or high-speed takedowns generates "Consignment Credits," which are used for high-tier O2-R0 upgrades.

<Callout type="info" title="Backer Influence">
Backers love a show. If you win a race without engaging in combat, your reward multiplier will be significantly lower than if you had performed at least three takedowns.
</Callout>
