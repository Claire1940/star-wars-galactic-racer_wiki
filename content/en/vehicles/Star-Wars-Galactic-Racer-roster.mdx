<Section icon={Bike} title="Vehicle Roster: Repulsorcraft Classes">

The true variety in the **Star Wars Galactic Racer roster** comes from the repulsorcraft themselves. Unlike traditional racing games, Galactic Racer utilizes a runs-based system where your choice of chassis fundamentally alters your tactical approach to a track. There are four primary classes available at launch, each designed for specific terrain and racing styles.

**Landspeeders** serve as the "tanks" of the racing world, offering weightier handling and a focus on drifting. They are ideal for surviving contact-heavy races where shunting rivals is necessary. **Speeder Bikes** offer incredible straight-line velocity but require precision timing for Kinetic Bursts to navigate corners. **Skim Speeders** are the technical choice, utilizing the "Knife Edge" trait for sharp banking turns. Finally, **Podracers** represent the pinnacle of speed, reserved for high-stakes events on planets like Tatooine.

| Vehicle Class | Key Trait | Handling Profile | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Landspeeder** | Drift | Heavy / Stable | Contact racing & learning lines |
| **Speeder Bike** | Kinetic Burst | Fragile / Fast | Open straights & risky overtakes |
| **Skim Speeder** | Knife Edge | Technical / Sharp | Winding tracks & urban circuits |
| **Podracer** | Extreme Thrust | High Skill / Fast | Iconic arena events & speed trials |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Speeder Bikes have the lowest durability in the roster. While they are tempting for their speed, a single poorly timed shunt from a Landspeeder can end your Galactic Tour run prematurely.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Expand Your Roster: Progression Guide">

Unlocking the full **Star Wars Galactic Racer roster** requires navigating the "Galactic Tour," a runs-based campaign structure. You do not start with every pilot or vehicle; instead, you must earn League Entry Tokens and Galactic Credits to purchase new chassis and recruit rivals to your cause.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Initial Qualifiers">
    Start the campaign as Shade. You will be given a basic Landspeeder to learn the mechanics of shunting and drifting on Jakku.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Earn Galactic Credits">
    Participate in standard races and 1v1 grudge matches. High placements and successful takedowns reward you with currency used at paddock vendors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock New Chassis">
    Visit vendors between tour legs to purchase Speeder Bikes or Skim Speeders. Each new chassis added to your roster opens up different branching paths on the map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Rival Pilots">
    Defeating legends like Sebulba in Scenario mode or specific Campaign events will often unlock their signature vehicle liveries or specialized parts for your own craft.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Roguelite Mechanics">
Remember that Star Wars: Galactic Racer is runs-based. If you crash out and lose your League Entry Token, you may lose some temporary upgrades, but your unlocked vehicle classes and Galactic Credits typically persist between attempts.
</Callout>

</Section>
