<Section icon={Target} title="How to Master New Circuits">

Learning a new map in Star Wars Galactic Racer requires a systematic approach. You cannot simply hold the boost button and expect to win. Follow these steps to decrease your lap times and increase your reputation within the Galactic League.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Reconnaissance">
    Run your first three laps in a durable Landspeeder. Don't focus on speed; focus on identifying the "Danger Zones" where acid, lava, or debris are most prevalent.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shortcut Identification">
    Every map has at least two major shortcuts. Look for paths that require a high-speed jump or a narrow squeeze through wreckage. These are often hidden behind destructible environment pieces.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Vehicle Tuning">
    Adjust your Repulsorcraft's handling for the specific planet. For Ando Prime, increase traction at the cost of top speed. For Tatooine, maximize cooling to offset the desert heat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Competitive Execution">
    Once the layout is memorized, switch to your preferred racing class (like a Podracer). Practice your "Shunt" timing to knock rivals into hazards during tight turns.
  </Step>
</Steps>
