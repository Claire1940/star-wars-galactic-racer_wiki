<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Modes and Campaign Structure">

The core experience is built around the Galactic Tour, a runs-based campaign structure where your survival is tied to your performance and League Entry Tokens. 

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Campaign Entry">
    Use a League Entry Token to begin a Galactic Tour. Each tour features branching paths with escalating difficulty.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Event Selection">
    Choose your path through the tournament. Higher-risk routes often yield better performance parts and Galactic Credits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Paddock Downtime">
    Between races, visit the paddock to talk with rivals, purchase vendor parts, and apply upgrades to your craft.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Takedown and Survive">
    Race aggressively to shunt and takedown rivals, but avoid crashing—losing too many times will revoke your entry token.
  </Step>
</Steps>
