<Section icon={Target} title="Campaign Progression and the Galactic Tour">

The core of your journey toward defeating **Star Wars Galactic Racer Kestar Bool** lies in the "Galactic Tour" mechanic. This is a runs-based system, meaning each attempt at the championship is unique. You start with a League Entry Token and must choose a path through various planets, each offering different rewards, risks, and environmental hazards.

Between races, you will visit the Paddock. This is a crucial downtime area where you can interact with other racers, listen to intel about Kestar's next move, and spend your hard-earned Galactic Credits on performance parts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure a League Entry Token">
    Enter the Galactic Tour by spending a token. This is your "life" for the run; if you crash out or fail to place in the top three consistently, the token is revoked.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate Branching Paths">
    Choose between different race types across Jakku, Tatooine, and Ando Prime. Some routes offer more credits, while others provide rare vehicle parts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade at the Paddock">
    Use the credits earned from qualifiers to improve your Afterburner, Battery, and Cornering stats. High-tier parts are essential for the Act 3 races against Kestar's elite squad.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Showdown">
    Reach the Derven Acos Arena with a fully optimized build to challenge Kestar Bool for the Galactic League trophy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Tip">
Don't hoard your Galactic Credits. Since the Galactic Tour is runs-based, spending on immediate upgrades like Afterburner efficiency can be the difference between finishing a run or starting over from Act 1.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Track Hazards and Planet Mastery">

The race for the championship takes you across the Outer Rim's most dangerous environments. Each planet presents unique challenges that Kestar Bool will attempt to exploit. Understanding the terrain is just as important as upgrading your engine.

**Confirmed Racing Locations:**
- **Jakku**: A dusty, wreckage-heavy landscape where visibility is low and debris can cause instant crashes.
- **Tatooine**: Home to the Mos Espa Circuit. Expect high-speed podracing sections with narrow canyons and treacherous shortcuts.
- **Ando Prime**: A frozen world where ice reduces traction, making the Landspeeder’s drift mechanic vital for survival.
- **Sentinel I**: A volcanic world where heat management for your Ramjet boost becomes a critical gameplay factor.
