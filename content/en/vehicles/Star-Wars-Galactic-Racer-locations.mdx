### Tatooine: The Boonta Eve Classic
Tatooine is the spiritual home of podracing [2]. Expect wide-open desert plains, treacherous canyon passes, and high-speed straights. It is the perfect track for testing the maximum velocity of your custom-built podracers, but watch out for Tusken Raider sniper fire in the narrower canyon sections.

### Jakku: Graveyard of Giants
Set in the aftermath of the Battle of Jakku, this track weaves through the decaying hulls of fallen Star Destroyers [2]. Racers must navigate tight interior corridors and avoid falling debris, making maneuverability and quick reflexes far more important than raw speed.

### Lantaana: The Volcanic Jungle
Lantaana is a brand-new addition to the *Star Wars* canon [2]. This tropical environment combines lush green jungles with active volcanic fissures. Racers must dodge lava flows and navigate through ancient stone temples, requiring a balanced vehicle setup that can handle both tight corners and sudden hazards.

### Track Optimization Process

Follow these essential steps to prepare your vehicle for any planetary environment before entering a ranked race [2].

  <Step num="1" title="Analyze Track Biome">

  <Step num="2" title="Select Vehicle Chassis">
