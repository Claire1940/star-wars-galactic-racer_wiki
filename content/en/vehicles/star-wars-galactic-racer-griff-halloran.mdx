<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Roguelite Campaign">

The single-player campaign in Star Wars Galactic Racer is not your traditional linear racing story. Instead, it adopts a **Roguelite structure** reminiscent of games like *Hades* or *Slay the Spire*. Players must navigate a node-based map, choosing between different events, challenges, and rewards. If you crash too many times during a "tour," your run ends, and you must start over from the beginning.

However, not all is lost upon failure. Certain perks, currency, and permanent unlocks carry over between runs, allowing you to gradually build a more powerful racer. This system ensures that every attempt feels productive, even if you don't reach the final confrontation with Kestor Boole on your first try.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Node Selection">
    Choose your path carefully on the Galactic League map. Some nodes offer high-risk races with massive payouts, while others provide safe havens for repairs and upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Event Participation">
    Compete in varied events, including standard races, elimination rounds, and time trials. Each event offers specific currency used for mid-run upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Managing Hazards">
    Dorvan Akos is filled with environmental hazards. Use your pilot's unique skills to avoid rockfalls, sandstorms, and sabotage from rival racers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Permanent Progression">
    After a run ends, visit Hibbi the Ardennian mechanic to spend your earned credits on permanent vehicle improvements and pilot perks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Permadeath Mechanics">
Crashing out of a race during a tour is permanent for that run. Always prioritize hull integrity over speed when navigating dangerous sectors.
</Callout>
