<Section icon={Swords} title="Boonta Eve Classic and the Ark Roose Rivalry">

The defining moment of Dud Bolt's career occurred during the 32 BBY Boonta Eve Classic on the desert planet of Tatooine. Entered alongside eighteen other elite competitors, Bolt started on the second row of the starting grid at the Mos Espa Grand Arena, positioned directly behind Sebulba. While his mechanic, Mat Rags, performed last-minute adjustments, the Troig commentators Fodesinbeed Annodue introduced him to the cheering crowd as "the mighty Dud Bolt."

The race proved to be highly chaotic. After falling toward the back of the pack during the first lap, Bolt was overtaken by Anakin Skywalker in the Notch. Bolt managed to fight his way back into contention, but his aggressive style proved to be his undoing in the third lap. While navigating the winding turns of the Coil, Bolt collided with the pilot Ark Roose. The resulting catastrophic crash sent both pilots to the Mos Espa medcenter and ended their hopes of victory.

This crash ignited a bitter, off-track rivalry. In a desperate bid for revenge, Roose attempted to poison Bolt, but accidentally poisoned Bolt's mother instead. Though she survived and fully recovered, the incident cemented their rivalry as one of the most intense in podracing history. Despite this setback, Bolt recovered and continued to compete across the galaxy, eventually racing on the icy tracks of Ando Prime in 22 BBY.
