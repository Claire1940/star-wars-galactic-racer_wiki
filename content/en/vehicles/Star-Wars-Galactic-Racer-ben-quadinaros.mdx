### Engine Tuning Priorities

When tuning your engines in the garage, prioritize the following sliders to maximize the potential of **Star Wars Galactic Racer ben quadinaros**:

1.  **Thrust Distribution**: Set to 60% Rear / 40% Front to improve turn-in response.
2.  **Fuel Mixture**: Lean (reduces top speed but significantly lowers heat buildup).
3.  **Brake Bias**: Rear-heavy to allow for aggressive drifting into tight corners.

### Racing Tactics by Lap

- **Lap 1**: Use your superior acceleration to get "Clean Air." Avoid the middle of the pack where Sebulba or Dud Bolt can ram your engines.
- **Lap 2**: Focus on heat management. Only boost on straightaways and keep your heat meter in the "Yellow" zone.
- **Lap 3**: This is where Ben's engines usually fail. If you have a lead, play defensively. If you are trailing, use the "Full Quad-Vent" to dump all heat and go for a final, risky 5-second boost.
