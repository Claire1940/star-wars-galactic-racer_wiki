Follow this step-by-step upgrade path to optimize your podracer for the mid-to-late game stages of the Galactic League campaign.

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boost Engine Output">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reinforce Hull Armor">

  </Step>
</Steps>
