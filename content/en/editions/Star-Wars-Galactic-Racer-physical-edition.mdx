<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Modes and Roguelite Campaign Structure">

The single-player campaign introduces a runs-based roguelite structure to the racing genre. Players step into the flight suit of Shade, an aspiring pilot climbing the ranks of the lawless Galactic League to challenge the formidable rival Kestar Bool. 

A standard campaign run, known as a Galactic Tour, forces players to make strategic decisions between races, balancing resource accumulation against track hazards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire a League Entry Token">
    Every competitive tour requires a League Entry Token to participate. Crashing out of races or failing to place in the top three can revoke your token, ending your current run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Chassis">
    Choose between three core repulsorcraft classes: the drift-friendly Landspeeder, the blisteringly fast Speeder Bike, or the highly technical Skim Speeder.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Navigate the Galactic Tour Map">
    Plot a course through branching event paths. Safer routes offer modest payouts, while high-risk tracks feature dangerous environments but reward massive Galactic Credits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade in the Paddock">
    Between competitive heats, spend your hard-earned Galactic Credits at paddock vendors. Install performance parts to upgrade your Afterburner, Battery, Cornering, and Ramjet systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Survive the Grand Finals">
    Reach the final arena in the circuit. Defeating rival pilots in high-stakes PvP grudge matches or chaotic podracing events secures permanent unlocks that carry over to future runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Overheating Management">
Your speeder features two distinct boost mechanics: the Afterburner for repeatable speed bursts, and the Ramjet for high-risk, aggressive shunts. Overusing the Ramjet will overheat your engines, leaving you vulnerable to rival takedowns. Use it strategically on straightaways.
</Callout>

</Section>
