<Section icon={Target} title="Galactic Tour: The Roguelite Gameplay Loop">

The core of Star Wars: Galactic Racer is the Galactic Tour, a solo campaign mode where players take on the role of Shade. Unlike traditional racers where you simply move from track to track, the Tour requires strategic decision-making and resource management.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire a League Entry Token">
    Every run requires a Token to enter the Galactic League. If you crash out or fail to meet placement requirements, the Token is forfeited, and you must start a new run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Path">
    The Tour features a branching map. You can choose high-risk routes for better rewards or safer paths to preserve your vehicle's integrity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Overheating and Damage">
    During races, using your **Ramjet** boost provides massive speed but increases heat. Excessive heat or physical collisions lead to permanent damage that persists between heats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit the Paddock">
    Between races, spend your earned Galactic Credits at the Paddock. Here, you can talk to NPCs, buy new parts, and apply permanent upgrades to your chassis.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Challenge the Rivals">
    At the end of each act, you must face a Rival in a 1v1 grudge match. Defeating them unlocks new chassis traits and progresses the story toward the final showdown with Kestar Bool.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mechanic | Description | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Afterburner** | Standard boost system | Used for maintaining top speed |
| **Ramjet** | High-risk kinetic burst | Essential for takedowns / Overheats craft |
| **Shunts** | Lateral physical attacks | Used to force rivals into hazards |
| **League Token** | Entry requirement | Lost upon tour failure |

<Callout type="info" title="Progression Persistence">
While individual runs can end in failure, players retain Galactic Credits and certain unlocked blueprints, allowing for stronger starts in subsequent attempts.
</Callout>

</Section>

The Galactic League spans several iconic and new locations across the Star Wars galaxy. Each planet introduces unique environmental hazards that require specific vehicle setups to navigate effectively. For instance, the slippery ice of Ando Prime demands higher cornering stats, while the dusty wreckage of Jakku requires robust shielding.

| Planet | Environment Type | Primary Hazard |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jakku** | Scrapyard Desert | Sharp wreckage / Sandstorms |
| **Tatooine** | Classic Desert | Narrow canyons / Sarlacc pits |

| **Lantaana** | Jungle / Forest | Dense foliage / Low visibility |
| **Sentinel I** | Volcanic | Lava flows / Rising heat levels |

The Mos Espa Circuit on Tatooine serves as a highlight for podracing fans, featuring high-speed straights and the infamous canyon turns. In multiplayer, these tracks support up to 12 players, with dynamic event triggers that can change the track layout mid-race.

<Callout type="tip" title="Vehicle Tuning">
Before entering a race on Ando Prime, prioritize **Cornering** and **Battery** upgrades. The low traction makes drifting difficult, so having precise control over your banking is more important than raw top speed.
</Callout>
