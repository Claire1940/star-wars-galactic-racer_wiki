<Section icon={Map} title="Game Overview & Hardware Requirements">

For PC players choosing the physical Collector's Edition route, verifying that your hardware can handle the high-speed rendering of **Star Wars: Galactic Racer** is essential. The game features fast-paced racing action across multiple diverse planets, including the frozen wastes of Ando Prime, the lush jungles of Lantaana, and the volcanic surfaces of Sentinel I.

The game's engine demands modern specifications to deliver smooth frame rates during intense 12-player online multiplayer races and high-speed solo Campaign runs.

| Component | Minimum PC Requirement | Target Performance Note |
|---|---|---|
| Operating System | Windows 10 (64-bit) | Required for modern API compatibility |
| Processor (CPU) | Intel Core i5-8400 / AMD Ryzen 5 2600 | Handles physics and AI calculations |
| Memory (RAM) | 12 GB RAM | Prevents stuttering during asset streaming |
| Graphics Card (GPU) | Nvidia RTX 2060 / AMD Radeon RX 5600 XT | Minimum for ray-traced lighting and reflections |
| Storage Space | 50 GB available space | **SSD strictly required** for fast track loading |
| Additional Input | Gamepad recommended | Highly recommended for precise steering control |

In addition to robust hardware, players will need to master the unique handling profiles of different vehicle classes. The Collector's Edition also gives you immediate access to the exclusive Deluxe Kor Sarun vehicle set.

| Vehicle Class | Primary Handling Identity | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| Landspeeder | Weighty handling, strong drifting focus | Managing tight corners and heavy contact |
| Speeder Bike | High straight-line speed, sensitive steering | Long straightaways and rapid overtaking |
| Skim Speeder | Hard banking turns, technical control | Winding tracks requiring precise lines |
| Podracer | Extreme speeds, fragile chassis | High-risk, high-reward racing on open circuits |
| Kor Sarun Deluxe Set | Exclusive high-performance custom builds | Showcasing premium style in online PvP lobbies |

<Callout type="warning" title="SSD Requirement">
Due to the speed at which repulsorcraft travel through the environments, standard mechanical hard drives (HDDs) are not supported. An SSD is required to prevent severe texture pop-in and loading stalls during races.
</Callout>
