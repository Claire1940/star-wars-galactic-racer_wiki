### Performance Tuning Categories
Between races in the Paddock, players can spend **Galactic Credits** on the following upgrades:
- **Afterburner**: Increases the duration and power of standard boosts.
- **Battery**: Improves energy regeneration for Ramjet bursts.
- **Cornering**: Enhances the responsiveness of banking and drifting.
- **Ramjet**: Increases the impact force of shunts and maximum top speed.
- **Chassis Traits**: Unlocks specific abilities like **Kinetic Burst** or **Drift** stabilizers.

<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner's Guide to the Galactic League">

Starting your career in the Outer Rim can be daunting. Follow these steps to ensure your first Galactic Tour doesn't end in the scrap heap.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starting Chassis">
    Begin with a **Landspeeder**. Its weightier handling and **Drift** trait make it much more forgiving when you inevitably collide with walls or rival racers during your first few runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Launch Boost">
    Timing is everything. Watch the countdown and input the **Engine and Shield** sequence perfectly to trigger a launch boost. This prevents you from getting swallowed by the pack in the first corner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Heat and Energy">
    Your **Afterburner** is for regular use, but the **Ramjet** should be reserved for straightaways or offensive shunts. Overusing the Ramjet will overheat your engines, leaving you vulnerable for several seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Tour Map">
    In Campaign mode, choose your route wisely. Some paths offer better **Galactic Credit** rewards, while others provide rare **Vendor Parts**. Always check the "Risk Level" before committing to a branch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prioritize Survival Over Speed">
    In a runs-based system, finishing 3rd is better than crashing out while trying to take 1st. You only have a limited number of **League Entry Tokens**; don't waste them on reckless maneuvers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Tour Failure">
If you crash too many times or fail to meet the minimum placement requirements, your tour ends. While you keep your **Galactic Credits**, you will have to start a new qualifier to earn a fresh Entry Token.
</Callout>

</Section>
