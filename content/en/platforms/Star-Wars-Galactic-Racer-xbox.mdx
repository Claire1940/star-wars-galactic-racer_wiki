<Section icon={Target} title="Star Wars Galactic Racer Beginner Guide">

Starting your career in the Galactic League can be daunting. The game's progression system, known as the Galactic Tour, requires players to manage a "League Entry Token." If you crash out too many times or fail to place in the top three, your token is revoked, and you must start a new tour. Follow these steps to ensure your first run is a success.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Landspeeder for Initial Runs">
    The Landspeeder's **Drift** trait makes it the most forgiving vehicle for beginners. Its weight allows you to survive collisions with aggressive AI pilots like Kestar Bool without spinning out immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Countdown Launch">
    Timing your accelerator input during the countdown provides a launch boost. Combine this with a quick shield input sequence to start the race with a full Afterburner meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Heat and Overheating">
    Using the **Ramjet** boost provides massive speed but generates heat. If you overheat, your engines will stall, leaving you vulnerable to takedowns. Use Ramjets only on long straights or for critical overtakes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invest in Cornering and Batteries">
    Between races, visit the Paddock vendors. Prioritize **Cornering** upgrades to handle the technical tracks of Ando Prime and **Battery** upgrades to increase your boost frequency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Aggressive Racing">
Don't be afraid to shunt rivals. Takedowns are a core mechanic of Galactic Racer. Removing a dangerous opponent early in the race can make the final lap much safer.
</Callout>

</Section>
