<Section icon={Map} title="Gameplay Modes and Rogue-lite Structure">

Unlike traditional arcade racers, this title introduces a runs-based "Galactic Tour" that functions as a rogue-lite campaign. Players take on the role of Shade, a rising pilot aiming to dethrone the legendary Kestar Bool. Each tour consists of branching paths where your choices determine the risks and rewards you face. Failing to place in the top three or suffering a total vehicle wreck can end your tour, forcing you to use a League Entry Token to start a fresh attempt.

| Mode Type | Player Count | Primary Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Campaign** | 1 Player | Story-driven runs-based Galactic Tour |
| **Scenario** | 1 Player | Specific challenge-based race formats |
| **Arcade** | 1 Player | Curated events with iconic racers |
| **PvP Grudge** | 2-12 Players | Competitive online reputation matches |
| **Multiplayer** | 2-12 Players | Cross-platform standard racing |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Tip">
In the Campaign mode, focus on "League Entry Tokens" as your most valuable resource. Losing these tokens resets your tour progress, though some Galactic Credits carry over for permanent upgrades.
</Callout>

The game emphasizes "contact racing," where shunting and takedowns are not just allowed but encouraged. The risk-and-reward system is tied to the Ramjet boost; while it provides incredible speed for overtaking or slamming rivals, it carries a high risk of overheating your engine, which can lead to a catastrophic failure in the middle of a tight turn.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner's Progression Guide">

Entering the Galactic League can be daunting for new pilots. The following steps outline the ideal progression path to ensure you don't burn through your League Entry Tokens too quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Launch Boost">
    Every race begins with a countdown. Timing your accelerator input alongside a specific engine-and-shield sequence will grant a launch boost, allowing you to clear the initial pack of racers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Cornering Upgrades">
    While top speed is tempting, the tracks on Jakku and Tatooine are filled with wreckage and narrow canyons. Improving your "Cornering" and "Battery" stats early on provides much better survivability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Learn the Overheat Threshold">
    Your Ramjet is a powerful tool for shunting rivals. However, using it until the gauge turns red will disable your engines. Practice "feathering" the boost to maintain speed without blowing your reactor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Arcade for Practice">
    Before risking a high-stakes Campaign run, use Arcade mode to familiarize yourself with the Mos Espa Circuit and the frozen tracks of Ando Prime. There is no penalty for crashing in Arcade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip">
If you are struggling with a specific track, switch to the Skim Speeder. Its **Knife Edge** trait allows for much tighter banking, making it easier to navigate the hazardous turns in the Derven Acos arena.
</Callout>

</Section>
