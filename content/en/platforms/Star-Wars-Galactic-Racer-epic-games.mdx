During a Galactic Tour run, you can upgrade these traits using Galactic Credits earned from successful placements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Chassis">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Performance Parts">
    Visit vendors between races to purchase Afterburner, Battery, and Cornering upgrades. These parts are essential for surviving escalating league challenges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Visual Liveries">

  </Step>
</Steps>
