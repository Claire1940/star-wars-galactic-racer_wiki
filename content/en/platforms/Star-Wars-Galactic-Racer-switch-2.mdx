<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Start Your Galactic Tour">

For players new to the rogue-lite racing genre, the initial hours of Star Wars: Galactic Racer can be punishing. Following a structured approach to your first few runs will help you accumulate the resources needed for permanent upgrades.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starting Chassis">
    Begin with the **Landspeeder**. Its weightier handling makes it more forgiving during the aggressive shunts and collisions common in the early League qualifiers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Launch Boost">
    Time your accelerator input with the countdown and follow up with a quick engine-and-shield sequence to trigger a launch boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Cornering Upgrades">
    In your first Paddock visit, spend your Galactic Credits on **Cornering** and **Afterburner** capacity. Pure speed is useless if you cannot navigate the wreckage on Jakku.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Heat and Energy">
    Use the Afterburner for repeatable boosts and save the Ramjet for straightaways or when you need to execute a Takedown on a rival.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Secure a Top Three Finish">
    In the early stages, focus on survival. A third-place finish keeps your League Entry Token active, while a "DNF" (Did Not Finish) can end your run prematurely.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Permadeath Mechanics">
While some resources like Galactic Credits can carry forward, a failed tour means losing your current vehicle's temporary performance parts. Always balance risk versus reward when choosing branching paths.
</Callout>

</Section>
