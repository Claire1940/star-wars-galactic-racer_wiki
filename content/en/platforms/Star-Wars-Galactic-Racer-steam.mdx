<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: Surviving Your First Tour">

Starting your career in the Galactic League can be overwhelming. Follow these steps to ensure your first few runs aren't cut short by a collision with a Jakku scavenger wreck.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Landspeeder Chassis">
    For your first run, the Landspeeder is the most forgiving. Its weight makes it less likely to be shunted off-track by aggressive AI, and its **Drift** trait helps you navigate corners while maintaining speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Launch Boost">
    Timing is everything. Listen for the countdown and input the engine and shield sequence perfectly to gain an immediate advantage. A clean start prevents you from getting caught in the "mid-pack" chaos where most crashes occur.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Your Overheat">
    The **Ramjet** boost provides incredible speed but generates massive heat. Use it only on long straights or when attempting a critical takedown. If you overheat, your engines will stall, leaving you vulnerable to being overtaken.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spend Credits on Cornering">
    In the early game, speed is secondary to control. Use your first Paddock visit to upgrade your **Cornering** stats. Staying on the track is more important than having the highest top speed if you can't make the turns on Ando Prime.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Permadeath Risk">
If you fail to place in the top three or crash out too many times, you lose your **League Entry Token**. This ends your current tour, and while you keep some credits, you will have to start the tour from the beginning.
</Callout>

</Section>
