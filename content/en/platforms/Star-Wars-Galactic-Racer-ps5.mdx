<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering the Galactic Tour">

Success in **Star Wars Galactic Racer ps5** requires more than just holding the accelerator. The game’s "runs-based" structure means that every race carries the risk of ending your tour. You must manage your "League Entry Tokens" carefully; losing too many races or crashing out completely will revoke your token, forcing you to start a new qualifier run.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Starting Chassis">
    Begin with the Landspeeder if you are new to combat racing. It has weightier handling and is more forgiving during collisions. As you gain confidence, switch to the Speeder Bike for pure speed or the Skim Speeder for technical, bank-heavy tracks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master the Launch Boost">
    Timing is critical at the starting line. You must synchronize your accelerator input with a specific engine-and-shield sequence during the countdown to trigger a launch boost, giving you an immediate lead.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Balance Afterburners and Ramjets">
    Use your Afterburner for repeatable, safe speed increases. Save the high-risk Ramjet for overtaking rivals or initiating takedowns. Be careful: overusing the Ramjet will overheat your engines, leaving you vulnerable for several seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invest in Paddock Upgrades">
    Between races, visit the Paddock to spend Galactic Credits. Prioritize "Cornering" and "Battery" upgrades early on. Better cornering allows you to maintain speed through tight turns, while battery upgrades increase your boost capacity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Tip">
Don't always aim for first place in every early race. In the Galactic Tour, finishing in the top three is often enough to progress. It is better to play safely and preserve your vehicle's integrity than to crash while chasing a risky lead.
</Callout>

</Section>
