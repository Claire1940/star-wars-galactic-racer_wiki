<Section icon={Target} title="How to Start Your Galactic Tour">

The core of the game is the Galactic Tour, a roguelite-inspired campaign where every decision matters. If you crash out or fail to place in the top three, your League Entry Token is revoked, and you must start a new run, though you retain some Galactic Credits and permanent unlocks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Chassis">
    Choose between a Landspeeder, Speeder Bike, or Skim Speeder. For beginners, the Landspeeder's **Drift** trait offers the most forgiveness during collisions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Branching Path">
    Review the Galactic League map. Some routes offer higher credit rewards but feature more hazardous environments like the volcanic surfaces of Sentinel I.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Launch Boost">
    Time your accelerator input with the countdown. A perfect engine-and-shield sequence at the start provides a crucial initial burst to pull away from the pack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Heat and Takedowns">
    Use shunts to knock rivals off course. Monitor your heat gauge; overusing the Ramjet will cause an engine stall, leaving you vulnerable in the lawless Outer Rim.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Spend Credits in the Paddock">
    Between races, talk to vendors. Invest in **Cornering** upgrades to handle the slippery ice of Ando Prime or the narrow caves of Tatooine.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Strategy">
In the early acts of the campaign, it is better to finish 3rd and survive than to risk a crash-out trying to take 1st place. Consistency keeps the tour alive.
</Callout>

</Section>
