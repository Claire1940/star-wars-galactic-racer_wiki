<Section icon={Target} title="Star Wars Galactic Racer Beginner Guide: Campaign Flow">

The heart of the experience is the single-player campaign, which functions as a "Galactic Tour." This mode is not a linear series of tracks; instead, it is a branching path where you must choose between different events, risks, and rewards. Each tour requires a League Entry Token. If you crash out or fail to meet the placement requirements too many times, your token is revoked, and you must start a new tour from the qualifiers.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering High-Stakes Racing Mechanics">

Success in the Galactic League requires more than just holding down the accelerator. You must master the balance between speed and vehicle health. The game features a "Contact Racing" philosophy, where shunting and taking down rivals is encouraged. However, every collision risks damaging your repulsorcraft, leading to potential engine failure or a total crash.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Perfect Launch">
    Time your acceleration with the countdown. Input the specific engine and shield sequence shown on screen to trigger a launch boost, giving you an immediate lead over the pack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Managing Boosters">
    Use the Afterburner for steady speed increases. Save your Ramjet for overtaking or shunting rivals, but watch the heat gauge carefully to avoid catastrophic overheating.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Tactical Takedowns">
    When a rival is alongside you, use a shunt to push them into track hazards. A successful takedown clears the way and often rewards you with a temporary boost or resource.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Management">
    Between races, decide whether to spend your Galactic Credits on immediate repairs or long-term performance upgrades like Battery capacity or Cornering efficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The heat management system is the most critical mechanic for advanced play. Pushing your craft to the limit with the Ramjet provides incredible speed but fills your overheat bar. If you hit the limit, your engine will stall, leaving you vulnerable to being overtaken or shunted by aggressive AI pilots like Sebulba.

| Maneuver | Description | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Afterburner** | Repeatable speed boost | Low |
| **Ramjet** | High-risk kinetic burst | High (Overheat) |
| **Shunt** | Side-swiping a rival | Medium |
| **Drift** | Controlled slide for corners | Low |
| **Knife Edge** | Sharp banking on skims | Medium |

<Callout type="warning" title="Overheating Hazard">
Do not use the Ramjet while cornering unless you have significant upgrades in the Cornering category. The sudden burst of speed often results in losing your racing line and smashing into the nearest canyon wall.
</Callout>

</Section>
