<Section icon={HardDrive} title="Storage Requirements and SSD Necessity">

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Optimize for the Galactic Tour">

If your PC barely meets the **Star Wars Galactic Racer system requirements**, following a specific setup routine can help you maintain the competitive edge needed to defeat rivals like Sebulba or Kestar Bool.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Graphics Preset">
    Start with the 'Medium' global preset. The game uses a dynamic resolution scaler by default; keep this enabled to prioritize frame rate over raw pixel count.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Memory Management">
    Close background applications like Chrome or heavy Discord overlays. With a 12 GB RAM minimum, the game is sensitive to memory leaks from external apps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="V-Sync and Latency">
    Disable V-Sync if you have a G-Sync or FreeSync monitor. Use 'Nvidia Reflex' if available to reduce the input lag between your controller and the repulsorcraft.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shader Pre-compilation">
    Allow the game to finish shader compilation on the main menu before starting your first Campaign run. This prevents stuttering during the first lap of a race.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Overheating Mechanic">
Note that "Overheating" is a gameplay mechanic for your speeder. Do not confuse in-game engine warnings with your actual PC hardware thermals, though high-speed racing will tax your GPU.
</Callout>
