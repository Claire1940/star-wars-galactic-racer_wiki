<Section icon={Map} title="Race Modes and Campaign Structure">

The game moves away from traditional linear progression, opting for a roguelite-inspired "Galactic Tour" system. You must manage your resources, reputation, and vehicle integrity to reach the end of the circuit.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start a Galactic Tour">
    Spend a League Entry Token to begin a run. Choose your path through branching event nodes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Race and Survive">
    Compete in standard races, elimination events, or 1v1 grudge matches. Poor placement or excessive crashes can end your tour early.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade and Repair">
    Use the paddock to install parts and repair your craft using rewards earned during the race.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mode | Players | Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Campaign** | 1 | Runs-based Galactic Tour progression |
| **Scenario** | 1 | Focused, standalone race challenges |
| **Arcade** | 1 | Curated events and vehicle showcases |
| **Multiplayer** | 2-12 | Competitive online PvP grudge matches |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important Mechanic">
The **League Entry Token** is the most valuable resource in your run. If you fail to place in the top three or sustain too much damage, you risk losing the token, forcing a reset of your current tour.
</Callout>
