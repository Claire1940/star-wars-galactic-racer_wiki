<Section icon={Swords} title="Repulsorcraft Classes and Physics">

The heart of *Star Wars: Galactic Racer* lies in its diverse array of repulsorcraft. Each class features distinct physics, handling characteristics, and strategic applications. Mastering the **star wars galactic racer beta** requires understanding which craft suits your personal racing style and the specific requirements of the track.

| Craft Class | Handling | Top Speed | Durability | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Speeder Bike** | Excellent | High | Very Low | Tight corners, quick acceleration |
| **Landspeeder** | Balanced | Moderate | Moderate | Consistent performance, beginner friendly |
| **Skim Speeder** | Drifty | Moderate | High | Rough terrain, slamming rivals |
| **Podracer** | Difficult | Extreme | Low | Long straights, high-stakes speed |

The physics engine differentiates between the light, twitchy movement of a Speeder Bike and the heavy, momentum-based drifting of a Skim Speeder. Podracers, while offering the highest speeds in the game, require immense skill to prevent the engines from overheating or detaching during high-G turns.

<Callout type="warning" title="Durability Warning">
Speeder Bikes can be easily destroyed by a well-timed "shunt" from a heavier Landspeeder. When piloting lighter craft, focus on evasion rather than direct combat.
</Callout>
