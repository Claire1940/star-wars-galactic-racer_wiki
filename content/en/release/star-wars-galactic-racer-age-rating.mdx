<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Interactive Elements and Online Features">

Beyond the offline story-driven campaign, *STAR WARS: Galactic Racer* features robust online components. Age rating agencies like the ESRB separate these interactive elements from the core content rating because player-to-player interactions cannot be fully predicted or controlled by the developers.

Parents should be aware of the following online features that may affect a child's gameplay experience:

- **Crossplay Multiplayer**: Players can compete against others across PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC.
- **Epic Online Services (EOS)**: Steam players are required to install and sign into an Epic Games account to manage non-Steam friends and enable crossplay.
- **Online Leaderboards**: While the game features leaderboards to compare times, they are restricted to friends rather than being fully public, reducing exposure to strangers.
- **Anti-Cheat Software**: The PC version utilizes Denuvo anti-tamper software to prevent cheating, which does not impact gameplay or rating.
