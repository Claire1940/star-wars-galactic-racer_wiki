<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Modes and Multiplayer Structure">

The **Star Wars Galactic Racer release** introduces a "runs-based" structure to the racing genre. In the **Campaign Mode**, you play as **Shade**, a rising star in the Galactic League. Your goal is to defeat **Kestar Bool** by surviving a series of escalating events known as the **Galactic Tour**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Chassis">
    Choose between a Landspeeder, Speeder Bike, or Skim Speeder. Each has a distinct handling trait like Drift or Kinetic Burst.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Tour">
    Use branching event paths to choose your next race. Some routes offer higher rewards but carry greater risks of crashing out.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade at the Paddock">
    Spend Galactic Credits between races to improve your Afterburner, Battery, and Cornering stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enter the Finals">
    Use your League Entry Tokens to access high-tier championships and challenge rival pilots for reputation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Multiplayer is another core pillar, supporting up to **12 players** in online PvP. The game features **cross-platform support**, allowing PC, Xbox, and PlayStation users to compete in the same lobbies. **PvP Grudge Matches** are specifically highlighted as high-stakes races where reputation points are on the line.

<Callout type="warning" title="Multiplayer Requirements">
PlayStation 5 users will require an active **PS Plus** subscription for online play, while Xbox users will need **Game Pass Core/Ultimate** to access the 12-player multiplayer modes.
</Callout>
